About the Summit关于论坛
Strengthening Australia–China Biomedical Collaboration推动中澳生物医学战略合作

This summit aims to strengthen strategic collaboration between Australia and China in biomedical sciences by bringing together national academy fellows, leading investigators, and senior research leaders to shape future research directions. The program will focus on key areas, including Infection and One Health, Immunity and Cancer, Biomedical Engineering and Drug Discovery, Epidemiology and Climate Change, and Artificial Intelligence in Biomedicine. As the inaugural biomedical summit, it will foster interdisciplinary dialogue and promote long-term collaboration between Australia and China.

本次峰会旨在通过汇集国家级院士、顶尖研究员和资深研究领导者，加强澳大利亚与中国在生物医学科学领域的战略合作，共同塑造未来研究方向。会议议程将聚焦以下关键领域：感染与同一健康、免疫与肿瘤、生物医学工程与药物研发、流行病学与气候变化，以及生物医学中的人工智能。作为首届生物医学峰会，它将促进跨学科对话，推动澳中之间的长期合作。
